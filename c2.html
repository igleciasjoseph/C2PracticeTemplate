What is an ASO?

Administrative Services Organization

C2 Platinum Inc.’s Administrative Services Organization (ASO) offers a transformative product that delivers company-wide HR support to businesses both large and small. Our ASO assumes three or more pivotal HR processes on behalf of clients, such as regulatory compliance, payroll, and benefits administration to ensure effective HR operational support. Our ASO products allow clients to eliminate certain transactional, repetitive, and other critically important HR tasks.
